Biography

Eiichiro Oda or the widely known as the man who created manga One Piece,born at 1 January 1975.

At the age of 17th, Oda create his first manga script called Wanted and get numerous award because of it. One of the award was the second place at Tezuka Award. At the age 19th oda starting his way to profesional mangaka carrier by being an manga maker assistant for some mangaka such as Suizan Police Gang (Shinobu Kaitani) and Rurouni Kenshin (Nobuhiro Watsuki).

At the same time, he also write down an opening arc of one piece, Romance Dawn. Without knowing that soon his manga would led him to succes as mangaka. Rigth now one piece has been sold more than 490 million copies around the world and become one of the most famous manga ever.
